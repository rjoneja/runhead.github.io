Runhead AC are a running club based in Peckham.

We run every Tuesday at 7.30 from the Beer Shop (40 Nunhead Lane) in 3 groups; a 4 mile group at 10 min/miles, a 5 mile group at 9 min/miles and a 6 mile group at 8 min/miles. On the last Tuesday of the month we lower the distance to 5k for each group so we can get back to the Beer Shop and have some food.
